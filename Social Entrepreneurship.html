As Muhammad Yunus of Grameen Bank rightly sums it up – “Social entrepreneurs are not content just to give a fish or teach how to fish. They will not rest until they have revolutionized the fishing industry.”Sankalp 16 provides a platform for social entrepreneurs and social enterprises to come forward to discuss socially inclined business plans, share ideas and achievements.

(ii) Chintan Bakshi
Chintan brings a unique experience of managing new ventures and start-up companies that address rural markets and are built around a rural ecosystem. He has over 9 years of experience leading ventures in the rural and the Bottom of the Pyramid (BoP) space. He has been working in the rural markets since 2004, developing innovative business models to commercialize grassroots technologies, foster rural entrepreneurship, provide internet connectivity / services, enable rural eCommerce and implement mobile enabled last mile distribution for FMCG. He has worked extensively in the rural markets of Gujarat, Orissa and Rajasthan.
