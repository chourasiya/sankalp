The Empresario, is the flagship event of SANKALP’ 2016, aiming to provide an opportunity to the start ups which are in their nascent stage to showcase their ideas and benefit from the feedbacks of our eminent panel of judges. The teams with a striking idea, an idea that uses science and technology for solving a social problem and is a change maker, can be chosen to be incubated by venture capitalists. INSPIRE- A path breaking idea not only solves an issue but it also inspires and motivates people to take up the challenge of social upliftment. We invite all the entrepreneurs to be that inspirational leader. INNOVATE- Science and technology used judiciously can do wonders for the society. Who would have thought in those days that humankind can be immune to many life taking diseases, but Louis Pasteur and Edward Jenner did!! From renewable sources of 0energy to organic farming, all have been a birth child of an innovative mind. So, why to restraint yourself now? INTEGRATE- Together we have and we will do miracles. We at Sankalp believe that by integrating all the noble efforts we can bring a synergistic impact. Thus we wish to bring together entrepreneurs and investors with like minds, the one those who believe that “Entrepreneurship is not just about making dimes, it’s also about bringing a positive change in the society.”
