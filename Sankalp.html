About us

Sankalp – a social convention is one of the many initiatives taken by the institute to inculcate the very habit of social service among the youth of the nation. It would host a plethora of events ranging from guest lectures byeminent personalities to competitions designed with the focus to create awareness among the masses.
Sankalp - suggests a resolution to come togetherand propose technical solutions to vanquish the country’s socio-economic problems. Our convention last year, acted as a platform for students from all over the nation to meet anddebate over ideas and initiatives for a betternation and it helped in raising awareness andgenerating bright ideas for handling thesocio-economic problems. Our mission is to motivate nation’s youth for being progressive citizens with a real passion for social service.
